Control Your Data.

Librebook's main feature is the freedom it gives to it's users. One of these freedoms is the ability it gives it's users to be forgotten. This page will explain how to delete a specific message you have sent on Librebook.

Deleteing Your Account

While it hurts to see you go, its an important foundation of Librebook that you can completely control your data and presence on Librebook. Below is the instructions on how to delete your account:

  1. Login to your Librebook account.
  2. Navigate to Delete Your Account from the box on the left on the main page.
  3. Then press the button labelled "Delete Account"
  4. Your profile and messages will all be deleted.Warning! This action is not reversible!
